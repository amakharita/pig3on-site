Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 20, 2026

Pig3on ("we," "our," or "us") operates the pig3on.io platform. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our service.

1. Information We Collect

When you use Pig3on, we may collect the following information:

  • Account information: Your name, email address, and phone number provided during signup.
  • Form submission data: Information submitted through forms connected to your Pig3on account, including names, email addresses, and message content from your customers.
  • SMS messages: The content of SMS messages exchanged between you and the Pig3on system for the purpose of drafting and sending emails.
  • Email content: Drafts and sent emails generated through the platform.
  • Usage data: How you interact with the service, including timestamps and feature usage.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to provide and improve the Pig3on service:

  • To deliver form submission notifications via SMS.
  • To generate email drafts using AI based on your instructions.
  • To send emails on your behalf through your connected email account.
  • To manage your phone number pool and message routing.
  • To improve the platform and fix issues.

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. We may share data with:

  • Service providers: Twilio (SMS delivery), Anthropic (AI email drafting), Google (email sending), and JotForm (form submissions) — only as necessary to operate the service.
  • Legal requirements: If required by law, regulation, or legal process.

4. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS) and secure authentication for all third-party integrations. However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. Form submission data and message logs are retained for up to 90 days after a conversation is completed and the number is released. You may request deletion of your data at any time.

6. SMS Messaging

By using Pig3on, you consent to receiving SMS messages at the phone number you provide. These messages are operational notifications related to form submissions, email drafts, and system status — not marketing. Message and data rates may apply. You can stop using the service at any time by contacting us.

  • Message frequency: Varies based on your form submission volume.
  • To opt out: Reply STOP to any message or contact us at adamdeanart@gmail.com.
  • For help: Reply HELP to any message or contact us at adamdeanart@gmail.com.

7. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time by emailing adamdeanart@gmail.com.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at adamdeanart@gmail.com.
