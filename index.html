Training
It gets better the more you use it
pig3on learns your voice from day one — and every correction, preference, and conversation makes it sharper. No settings page. No onboarding wizard. Just use it.
Upload past emails to jumpstart your voice. Feed it sales frameworks, scripts, or reference material. Every rule you set sticks permanently — building a smarter assistant with every interaction.

Don't like a word it used? Tell it once and it never uses it again. Every correction is permanent — pig3on builds a running set of rules unique to you.

Onboarding

Start with your real voice

Export your sent emails as a CSV and upload them — pig3on learns your tone, phrasing, and personality before you even send your first message. You can also upload sales books, scripts, or anything you want it to reference.

I uploaded 200 of my sent emails
Analyzed. I'll match your tone — direct, warm, no filler. I noticed you never use "dear" or "sincerely." Noted.
